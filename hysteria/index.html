What to expect?

Hysteria is a journey of the emotions from despair to rage told through a story of a woman losing her grasp of reality.
The show is accompanied by a live performance of atmospheric blackened gothic doom metal music, and will please fans of such icons as Cult of Luna, Swallow the Sun, Enslaved, and Lacuna Coil.
This 21+ show is touching delicate themes of pain, death and mental disturbance, emphasized with unsettling, uncomfortable and artistic imagery to enhance the conveyance of extreme emotions. Intense lighting will not be safe for those with epilepsy and other conditions with sensititvity to light.
Our experienced musicians have been performing in the metal scene in PNW with:

  • Dual Analog
  • Kerr Ring
  • No Living Witness
  • Random Rockers

  • Vesuvian

The story

Time passed in an indistinguishable wave, as the first witnessed The Breaking of Lena, the awakening of her sisters, and the true glory of The Maze as it took shape around them.
The Maze called Lena back in with her every waking thought, her every breath.
It glitched as it rose, the infinite possibilities of tile and metal and wood and grass flickering with horrible images between, blackened eyes crying gore and teeth growing out of palms and the pained and choked screaming of someone she must have loved once when she was Lena.
Names gave form. Names gave meaning. Names gave expectations. The sisters had none of those.
